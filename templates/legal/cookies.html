Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 1, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how Jamarik Technologies Ltd uses them on our website, and how you can control your cookie preferences.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, phone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They help websites remember your preferences, understand how you use the site, and improve your experience.

2. Cookies We Use

2.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the website to function. They cannot be disabled.

  • csrftoken: Django's Cross-Site Request Forgery protection token. Expires with the session.
  • sessionid: Maintains your session state. Expires when you close your browser.
  • jmk_cookie_consent: Remembers your cookie consent choice. Expires after 1 year.

2.2 Analytics Cookies (with consent)

These help us understand how visitors interact with our website so we can improve it.

  • _ga / _gid: Google Analytics cookies tracking page views and user behaviour. Expire after 2 years / 24 hours.

2.3 Functional Cookies (with consent)

These cookies enable enhanced functionality such as remembering your preferences.

  • jmk_prefs: Stores UI preferences. Expires after 6 months.

3. Third-Party Cookies

Some pages may embed third-party content (e.g., maps, videos) that may set their own cookies. We do not control these cookies. Refer to the respective third parties' privacy policies for details.

4. How to Manage Cookies

You can control cookies in several ways:

  • Cookie banner: When you first visit our site, use the banner to accept or decline non-essential cookies.
  • Browser settings: Most browsers allow you to block or delete cookies in Settings → Privacy. Note that blocking all cookies may affect website functionality.
  • Opt-out tools: Google Analytics opt-out is available at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

5. Do Not Track

Our website currently does not respond to "Do Not Track" signals from browsers, though we honour your explicit cookie consent choices made via our banner.

6. Updates to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy as we add or remove cookies from our website. Changes will be reflected by updating the "Last updated" date above.
