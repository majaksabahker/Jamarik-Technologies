Our Story

Built in Africa,
for Africa

Jamarik Technologies was founded on a simple belief: that world-class technology should not be imported it should be built here, by us, for our people.

Mission

Why We Exist

We exist to close the technology gap between East Africa and the rest of the world one intelligent system at a time. From Juba to Kampala, from clinics to government offices, we build software that works in low-bandwidth environments, supports local languages, and integrates with mobile money.

Our work is not theoretical. Every product we ship solves a documented problem faced by real people in our communities.

Vision

To become South Sudan's leading software and artificial intelligence company, empowering organizations across East Africa and beyond with innovative digital solutions that drive growth, operational efficiency and sustainable digital transformation.

Mission

To empower businesses, institutions and communities through innovative software, artificial intelligence and digital solutions that enhance efficiency, accelerate growth, and drive sustainable transformation, while contributing to the digital future of South Sudan, East Africa and beyond.

A Message from Our CEO

A Vision Rooted in African Excellence

"

When I founded Jamarik Technologies, I wasn't just starting a company I was making a statement. A statement that South Sudan, East Africa and the entire continent has the talent, the drive and the intellect to build world-class technology from within.

For too long, African nations have been consumers of technology built elsewhere, designed for contexts that don't match our realities. At Jamarik, we flip that narrative. Every system we architect is built with African infrastructure constraints in mind intermittent power, limited bandwidth, mobile-first users, and multilingual communities.

We are not chasing the Silicon Valley playbook. We are writing our own one that centres local knowledge, community impact and sustainable innovation. Our AI models are trained on African data. Our apps work offline. Our team is from here.

If you are a business an NGO or a government agency that believes technology should work for your people we are ready to build that future together.

Jamarik Sabahker
Founder & CEO, Jamarik Technologies Ltd
